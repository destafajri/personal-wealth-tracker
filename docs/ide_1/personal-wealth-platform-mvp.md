# Personal Wealth Platform — MVP Idea Framework

## 1. Core Concept & Goals

A privacy-first, manual-input personal finance and wealth tracking platform focused on asset allocation visibility and delivering instant financial health insights.

---

## 2. Asset Management Module

Users enter their owned assets, which are automatically categorized into two main groups based on liquidity level:

### Liquid Assets
Assets that can be quickly converted to cash with minimal value loss:
- Savings / Cash (user can input USD and IDR.. also coverted into IDR if user input USD and integrated with live USD to IDR)
- Time Deposits
- Gold / Precious Metals (user can input manual the gramation and also calculated into IDR, Integrate with live gold price such as pegadaian digital, xausd, antam, ubs, etc..)
- Stocks & Mutual Funds (user can choose indonesian or US stocks and input manual every stocks code and quantity or lots, Integrate with live price with indonesian stocks or US stocks)
- Government Securities (Bonds)
- Crypto Assets (Integrate with live crypto price)

### Non-Liquid Assets
Assets that take longer to sell or convert to cash:
- Property (Land, House, Apartment)
- Vehicles (Car, Motorcycle)
- Pension Funds (e.g., BPJS Employment, DPLK)

---

## 3. Cash Flow & Liabilities Module (Income, Expenses, and Debt)

This module separates monthly cash flow obligations from total outstanding debt that weighs against net worth:

- **Income**: Monthly salary, dividends, deposit interest, rental income, etc.
- **Liabilities / Total Outstanding Principal**: Total remaining debt (e.g., remaining mortgage principal, total outstanding credit card / buy-now-pay-later balances). This figure is mandatory as a deduction from total assets.
- **Expenses**:
  - *Fixed Expenses*: Monthly debt installments, rent, utility bills, internet subscription.
  - *Variable Expenses*: Average monthly spending (food, transportation, lifestyle).

---

## 4. Analytics & Insights Dashboard Module

After the user clicks the "Track" or "Calculate" button, the platform acts as an automated financial advisor and displays the following metrics:

- **Net Worth**: Calculated as Total Assets minus Total Liabilities.
- **Debt Service Ratio (DSR)**: The percentage comparing total monthly debt installments to monthly net income. The system displays green if the ratio is safely below 30%, and a warning color if it exceeds the 40% threshold.
- **Financial Runway (Emergency Fund)**: Dynamically calculated by dividing Total Liquid Assets by Total Monthly Expenses. The system evaluates whether the user meets the ideal threshold (e.g., 3–6 months of expenses for singles, or 9–12 months for those with a family).
- **Savings Rate**: The percentage of income successfully saved or invested each month.

---

## 5. Visualization & Export Module (Output)

- **Allocation Chart (Pie / Donut Chart)**: Visualizes the percentage breakdown of asset distribution (e.g., 40% Stocks, 30% Property, 30% Cash).
- **Sankey Diagram**: An interactive flow chart visualizing the journey of incoming cash flow — from primary income sources branching out into various expense categories, debt repayments, and savings.
- **Export Report**: A button to download the full asset summary table, ratio metrics, and cash flow history as a Spreadsheet (CSV / Excel).

---

> This framework evolves the app from a simple "asset recorder" into a "financial health analyzer" — highly sought after by working professionals and millennials today.
